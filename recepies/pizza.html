Pizza Recepie

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

When you finish making this pizza consider adding some pineapple on top for extra flavor

Ingridients

  • bread
  • pizza sauce
  • mozzarella
  • oven

Steps

  1. Begin my spreading the pizza sauce on the bread
  2. take mozzarella and spread it evenly all across the sauce
  3. carefully take the pizza and put it in the oven
  4. Depending on individual oven pizza cooking time may vary, check after 8 minutes and adjust accordingly until cooked
  5. Take out the pizza and let it rest
  6. Enjoy!
